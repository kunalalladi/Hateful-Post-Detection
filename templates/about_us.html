About Us

What's this project about?

We are using natural language processing and computer vision. The goal is to analyze meme captions along with the associated image in the background by considering the context to ultimately classifying a meme as hateful or not.

Why did we do this?

We did this project as a part of a college curriculum (Mini-Project). Out of the many ideas, we found this project to be interesting and stood apart from the usual ideas.
